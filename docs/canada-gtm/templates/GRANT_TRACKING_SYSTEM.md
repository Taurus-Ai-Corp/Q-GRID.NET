## Risk Management

### Grant-Specific Risks

| Risk | Grant Affected | Mitigation |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| FNBC letter delayed | IRAP, Indigenous Innovation | Early outreach, alternative partners |
| Application rejected | All | High-quality applications, multiple grants |
| Approval slower than expected | All | Bridge financing, phased pilot |
| Compliance issues | All | Monthly compliance reviews |
| Partner withdrawal | Indigenous Innovation | Multiple community partnerships |

### Contingency Plans

**If IRAP Rejected**:
- Redirect resources to Indigenous Innovation (larger amount)
- Apply to alternative NRC programs
- Accelerate revenue generation from pilot

**If Indigenous Innovation Rejected**:
- Apply to Indigenous Services Canada programs
- Seek private foundation funding
- Scale pilot with FNBC revenue share only

**If Both Stage 1 Grants Rejected**:
- Pursue smaller Provincial grants
- Seek angel/seed investment
- Reduce pilot scope to 1 community
