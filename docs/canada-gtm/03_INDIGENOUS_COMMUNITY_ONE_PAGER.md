## **4-STEP HOW IT WORKS**

### Step 1: Download (5 minutes)
Download Q-GRID app on Android/iPhone smartphone
- Free to download
- 20MB app size (works on older phones)
- Available in English + Indigenous languages

### Step 2: Verify Identity (87 seconds)
Quantum-safe KYC verification
- Community member verifies with phone number
- Live face verification (respecting Indigenous privacy frameworks)
- $5 one-time fee (vs $150-300 traditional)
- Community keeps the data (not sold to data brokers)

### Step 3: Create Wallet (30 seconds)
Activate offline payment wallet
- Funds load from your bank account (secured through FNBC)

Send payments community-wide
- **Offline**: Bluetooth mesh to nearby phones (no internet needed)
- **Online**: Email/phone number anywhere in Canada
- **Merchants**: QR code at community store/restaurant
